Biography: I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. I received the PhD degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, in June 2022. My research interests include computer architecture, storage system, embedded system, and processing-in-memory.
